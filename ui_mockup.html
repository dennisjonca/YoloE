How to Use Visual Prompting:

  1. Stop inference if it's running
  2. Click "Capture Snapshot" to capture the current frame
  3. Click and drag on the snapshot to draw bounding boxes around objects
  4. Click "Save Snapshot with Boxes" to configure the model
  5. Wait for model to re-export and warm up (~2 minutes)
  6. Click "Start Inference" to track similar objects
